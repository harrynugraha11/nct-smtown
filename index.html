Neo Culture Technology

NCT (Korean: 엔시티; an acronym for Neo Culture Technology) is a South Korean boy band formed by SM Entertainment and
introduced in January 2016. The group currently consists of 23 members and divided into five different sub-units: NCT U, NCT 127,
NCT Dream, WayV, and NCT DoJaeJung. In South Korean charts, NCT has recorded more than 27 million album sales across all sub-units,
making them the best-selling act under SM Entertainment.
